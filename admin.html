Setup Guide

  1. Create an account at EmailJS.com
  2. Add an email service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  3. Create email templates for inquiries and bookings
  4. Copy your Service ID and Public Key to the fields above
  5. Copy your template IDs to the template fields
  6. Save your changes and test the forms on your website
